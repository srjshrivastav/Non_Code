#Method to Change the Default folder For Desktop 


Step 1: Go to the home.

Step 2:Press ctrl+H to show Hidden files

Step 3:Now open .Config Folder

Step 4:Now open "user-dirs.dirs" files in any editor.

Step 5:Now change the Following line :
		
	XDG_DESKTOP_DIR="$HOME/Desktop"----->XDG_DESKTOP_DIR="$HOME/Your_Folder_Name"
Step 5:Restart Your System
